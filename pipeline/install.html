3 · How to use

  1. Show your bookmarks bar (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + B).
  2. Drag both buttons above onto it. (If dragging won't stick, click Copy, then make a new bookmark and paste the copied text as the URL.)
  3. Open Discord in your browser (not the desktop app — bookmarklets need a browser tab).
  4. On a geographic channel, click 🔥 Capture Channel. Inside a drop thread, click 🧵 Capture Thread.
  5. Scroll up to load older messages, then click again — re-clicks never create duplicates.

A green banner confirms how many messages were sent. (The send is fire-and-forget, so a green banner means "sent", not "stored" — verify rows in the Sheet the first few times.)
